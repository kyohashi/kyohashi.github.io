About

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Tsukuba and a Staff Data Scientist at Indeed. My research focuses on statistical modeling and its applications to marketing science, including causal inference, Bayesian methods, and mathematical optimization. I received my M.S. and B.S. in Statistics from Tokyo Institute of Technology.
